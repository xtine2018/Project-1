PRINCIPAL DESIGNER

Marco DiMaccio is an award winning architectural designer who brings a playful yet practical approach to the world of design. With a background in architecture, product design, and film work, DiMaccio developed PUNCHouse Design Group - a company dedicated to creating noteworthy architecture, landscape design, furnishings and product design.

DiMaccio received his formal education from Rhode Island School of Design and Art Center College of Design. His accomplishments span an impressive range, all of which have inspired and informed his highly honed eye for all aspects of design. Known for his rich modernist style, DiMaccio’s designs are a beautiful balance of playful modernism and creative efficiency earning him numerous innovation in design awards and featured on HGTV, E!News, Entertainment Tonight, and multiple national publications.

Projects include an award winning sustainable home for Lisa Ling which achieved a (USGBC) LEED for Homes Platinum Certification, Multi-platinum recording artist Anastacia’s Beverly Hills home, Lakeshore Entertainment Beverly Hills Film offices, the Beastie Boys - Grand Royal Records production space, and numerous commercial companies and private residences. HIs work as a Production Designer in Hollywood has also garnered high praise including Clio and ADDY awards. He has collaborated with leading commercial and Academy Award winning film directors, has designed over 500 commercials, music videos for mega stars, Rhianna, JLO, Elton John, Ozzy Osborn, and Andrea Bocelli.
